Be the change

Everyone can help limit climate change.From the way we travel,to the electricity we use and the food we eat,we can make a difference. Trees absorb carbon dioxide,so planting more trees can help reduce the amount of carbon in our atmosphere.

How can we make a difference?

By being responsible human beings,we can make a difference

This website will help people to become more responsible by spreading awareness. Global warming stresses ecosystems through temperature rises,water shortages and increased fire threats. The world's roads are clogged with vehicles, most of them burning diesel or petrol. Walking or riding a bike instead of driving will reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
